Definition of administrator

1. a person legally vested with the right of administration of an estate

2. one who administers especially business, school, or governmental affairs

a person who manages a computer network or system

3. a priest appointed to administer a diocese or parish temporarily

More Definitions for administrator

a person whose job is to manage a company, school, or other organization

a person who controls the use of something (such as property or money)

Legal Definition of administrator

a person appointed by a probate court to manage the distribution of the assets in the estate of a person who has died without leaving a valid will or leaving a will that does not name an executor able or willing to perform
